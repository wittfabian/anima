The ancestor

Two rules — a live cell with 2 or 3 neighbours survives; a dead cell with exactly 3 is born — and from them: gliders, guns, oscillators, computers. Devised by John Horton Conway in 1970, it is the great-grandparent of every world in this cabinet. Draw on the void to seed life.
